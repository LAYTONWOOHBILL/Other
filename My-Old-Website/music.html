MUSIC 🐶

Layton Wu is a Taiwan-raised, Chicago-based songwriter and producer. His passion lies in Jazz, Soul, Boogie, RnB and Hip-Hop from the 1970s to the present day. Experimenting with beat-making in Beatmakers Taipei, he befriended fellow producers Sonia Calico, qiubo and Conehead.After two self-released mixtapes, several singles were featured on  UnderU (Taipei), Babel Records (China) and Tokyo Dawn Records (Germany) compilations, and he has lent production talent to the likes of 9m88. Lately, he has been working on several singles and his debut album.

雷頓狗 ，音樂風格深受Jazz, Soul, Boogie, RnB, Hip-Hop 影響，於2016年受台灣的Beatmakers 啟發 Sonia Calico/ 秋波/ Conehead錐頭，進而開始創作Instrumental Beat和臥室音樂。 接著發行了兩張 Beat Tape，於2017年『A Way Into Your Heart』單曲收錄於台灣廠牌 UnderU 《GREENHOUSE》合輯 , 接續收錄於中國廠牌 Babel Records 《Swimming Pool》合輯 和德國廠牌 Tokyo Dawn Records 《The Boogie Volume 6》合輯 。 於2018年，開始與 9m88 有多次的合作和摯友們 秋伯樂，共同製作《快樂跨下》專輯，目前旅居芝加哥，已發行數首單曲和Mixtapes在各大平台。 雷頓狗的命名本是一隻住在美國的台灣米克斯，希望吸收融合各國的文化，音樂。也就如同台灣一樣，來創作出屬自己的音樂。
